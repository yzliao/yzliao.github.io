Yizheng Liao

I am the Leavell Fellow and a Ph.D. candidate in the Civil and Environmental Engineering Department at Stanford University. Currently, I am working with Prof. Ram Rajagopal and Prof. Anne Kiremidjian. Before studying at Stanford University, I received the M.S. degree and B.S. degree, both in Electrical and Computer Engineering, from Worcester Polytechnic Institute in 2011.

Research Interest

  • Statistical Learning and Data Analytics

  • Statistical and Graphical Model

  • Wireless Sensor Network

  • Structural Health Monitoring

  • Smart Grid

  • Change Point Detection
