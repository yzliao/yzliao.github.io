Yizheng Liao

I am a Visiting Scholar in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at Stanford University. Also, I am a Data Scientist (Machine Learnings Track) at Airbnb. At Stanford, I am working with Prof. Ram Rajagopal and Prof. Anne S. Kiremidjian.

Research Interest

  • Urban System and Infrastructure System

  • Machine Learnings and Statistical Learnings

  • Wireless Sensor Networks

  • Infrastructural Health Monitoring

  • Distribution Power Systems

  • Change Point Detection

Recent News

  • September 2018: Our paper “Urban MV and LV Distribution Grid Topology Estimation via Group Lasso” has been accepted by IEEE Transacations on Power Systems.

  • September 2018: Our paper “Structural Damage Detection and Localization with Unknown Post-Damage Feature Distribution Using Sequential Change-Point Detection Method” has been accepted by ASCE Journal of Aerospace Engineering.

  • September 2017: We present our papers “Experimental validation of an improved dynamic displacement estimation method using combined accelerometer and gyroscope sensors” and “Use of Time-frequency Damage Sensitive Features for Structural Damage Diagnosis” in International Workshop of Structural Health Monitoring (IWSHM 2017) at Stanford, CA.
